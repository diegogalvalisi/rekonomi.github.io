About Us

We are a collective of activists and researchers committed to re-thinking future-focused economy— macroeconomic models, innovative alternative enterprises, and bridging the Kosovo reality with the broader international context

Mission

What We Do.

As an organization and event space, dedicated to discussing, researching, and developing knowledge around various topics related to rethinking and re-developing the economic system for the future

Macro-models

Refere to the wider ideas and higher-level systems that encompass all the economic factors and organizations, such as geoeconomics, post-capitalist ideas, monetary theory, state public policy, and many other abstract conceptualizations

Alternative enterprises

Alternative novel non-private organizations that are dedicated to the the production, distribution of goods and services- like not-for-profit enterprises, cooperatives, for-benefit companies, community owned-business, state companies, and others similar

Public infrastructure

Improvement of current public infrastructure, like applied goverment technology, public places and spaces, better transport system, up to date education institutions, social housing, and many other related topics

Change strategies

Explore insights and possibilities related to how to implement these new models and organizations- involving topics like non-state strategies of change, macro structural analysis, capacities development strategies, and so on

Frameworks

We follow certain practical principles related to network approach, glocalism, participative open-governance, not-for-profit, high-tech application, ethics-based functioning, and an emergent-adaptive dynamic reference
